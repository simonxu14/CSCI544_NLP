This is an engaging young adult novel. The plot is fast-paced and the author dives right into the main conflict. The characters are a bit thin at times. This dystopian novel kept me reading, but the story-line would be even better if it were written for adult readers. Not too much is given away on how Panem became the new capitol, but the book does raise interesting themes of how media influences our lives and actions.<br /><br />Katniss is a great first-person narrator, but other aspects of this dystopian world could have been developed into much richer thematic material.<br /><br />[...]
